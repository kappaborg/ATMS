## 🚀 Quick Import Guide

### **Step 1: Locate Diagram**
Navigate to: `docs/technical/UML/[Category]/[Diagram Name].png`

### **Step 2: Import to Canva**
1. In Canva, click "Uploads" (left sidebar)
2. Click "Upload media"
3. Select your PNG file
4. Drag to slide

### **Step 3: Adjust (if needed)**
- Resize to fit slide
- Add title overlay if diagram doesn't have one
- Add annotations/callouts for emphasis
- Keep original design - it's professional!

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Use High-Resolution Versions**: Your PNGs are already high quality - perfect for presentation!

2. **Maintain Consistency**: Use diagrams from the same style/category together

3. **Add Titles**: If diagrams don't have titles, add them in Canva

4. **Don't Overcrowd**: Use 1-2 diagrams per slide maximum

5. **Callouts**: Add text boxes to highlight key parts of diagrams

6. **Color Match**: Your diagrams already match - no need to recolor

7. **Backup Slides**: Keep optional diagrams ready for Q&A section
